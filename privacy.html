Privacy Policy

Horse Incident Reporter respects your privacy. We only collect the information you provide while submitting an incident report, such as:

  • Incident category and description
  • Location and time (if you allow)
  • Optional carriage/horse/operator details
  • Optional photos
  • Optional contact info (name, email, phone)

This information is used solely for generating an email report that you send using your Gmail account to the designated recipients.

We do not read, store, or share your Gmail data. Your data remains on your device and inside the email you send.

If you have questions, please contact us at support@example.com.
